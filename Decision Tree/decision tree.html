We'll calculate first entropy of traffic class(Yes/No).

P(Yes) = 11 N(No) = 14

Entropy(Class-traffic) = -P/(P+N) log(P/(P+N)) - N/(P+N)log(N/(P+N)) Note: All logs refer to base 2.
